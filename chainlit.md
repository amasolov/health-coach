# Health Coach

Your AI-powered fitness coaching assistant. Ask me about:

- **Training load** — current CTL/ATL/TSB and form status
- **Activities** — recent workouts, performance trends
- **Body composition** — weight, body fat, muscle mass trends
- **Vitals** — resting HR, HRV, sleep, stress
- **Strength** — Hevy workout history and progress
- **Treadmill workouts** — generate zone-based iFit workouts
- **Goals and action items** — track progress toward your targets

Sign in with Google or your username to get started.
